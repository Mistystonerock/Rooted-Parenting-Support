Home

Create your account or sign in

Parents and caregivers can use this page to create an account, sign in, and then set up their family profile inside the app. If an agency gave you an invite code, enter it here first.

Parent Sign In

After sign in, you will be taken to the family setup page so you can add caregiver and child information.

What happens next

  • Parent creates or signs into an account.
  • If the family has an agency invite code, it is saved for family setup.
  • Parent completes family setup with caregiver name and child names.
  • Family profile is saved into Supabase instead of only on the device.
  • When a valid invite code is used, the family is connected to the agency automatically for read-only CPS or court review.

Shared program setup

  • Parents create their own account and family profile.
  • Multiple children can be saved under the same family record.
  • Daily logs, assessments, and completion records can later feed the shared program portal.
  • Staff can review assigned families in the read-only portal after case assignment.
