Rooted Parenting Network

A trauma-informed family support platform for parents, teachers, and providers

Rooted Parenting Network helps caregivers, teachers, and professionals respond to children with trauma history and behavior concerns using one shared, trauma-informed support system. The platform combines daily guided lessons, real-time response tools, family-provider messaging, shared goals, private documents, trauma assessment tracking, and certificate-based completion for home, school, CPS, court, therapy, and community-based family services.

21-Day Program

Daily lessons, reflections, quizzes, and skill-building check-ins.

Shared Team Tools

Messaging, shared goals, child support plans, schedule tracking, and trauma assessments.

Parent and Agency Ready

Mobile-ready tools, secure family records, and practical support across systems.

  • Parents and caregivers of children with behavioral or emotional challenges

  • Providers, therapists, counselors, and case workers coordinating support
  • CPS-involved and reunification families
  • Juvenile court and diversion referrals
  • School-linked family support programs
  • Family preservation, stabilization, and behavioral health programs

  • Trauma-informed and practical, not shame-based
  • Built around daily use, not just handouts, PDFs, or reading
  • Keeps parents, teachers, and providers aligned around the same response plan
  • Supports both parent growth and professional documentation
  • Structured enough for referral, monitoring, confidential file sharing, and certificate completion
  • Works for both direct parent support and agency implementation

Course Tracks

Track 1

CPS Positive Parenting and Reunification

Supports parent skill-building, stability, trust rebuilding, emotional safety, and structure during reunification or active child welfare involvement.

Track 2

Behavior Support for Parents

Focuses on defiance, aggression, anxiety, school struggles, calm consequences, positive reinforcement, and safer parent responses.

Track 3

Court-Ordered Parenting Skills

Provides structured parent education with progress tracking, participation documentation, and certificate-based completion.

Track 4

Teacher and School Trauma Support

Helps teachers respond to trauma-related behavior, school stress, de-escalation needs, and trauma/ADHD overlap with practical classroom supports.

Professional Features

  • Printable documentation, reports, and certificates
