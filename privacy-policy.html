Rooted Parenting Network is committed to respecting the privacy of parents, caregivers, children, educators, and referral partners using this website and app-based support material.

The current app stores progress information, worksheet entries, assessments, and form entries locally on the user’s device or browser. This means the information stays on that device unless the user shares or prints it.
