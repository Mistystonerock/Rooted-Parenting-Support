Privacy Policy

Rooted Parenting is committed to respecting the privacy of families, caregivers, and community users.

This website is intended to provide educational information, printable resources, and parenting support content. At this time, Rooted Parenting does not collect personal health information through the website unless a user voluntarily provides information through direct communication.

Information Use

  • Content is provided for educational and support purposes.
  • No confidential or clinical relationship is created by using this website.
  • If contact forms or email communication are added in the future, users should avoid sending sensitive personal health information unless specifically requested through a secure process.

Third-Party Links

This website may include links to printable documents or outside platforms. Rooted Parenting is not responsible for the privacy practices of outside websites.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as the program grows. Continued use of the website means users accept the current version of this policy.
