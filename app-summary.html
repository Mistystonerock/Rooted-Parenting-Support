Rooted Parenting Support

One Page Summary

The Rooted Parenting Support Program is designed to help parents build safe, stable, and supportive environments for children through practical tools, self-reflection, and skill building.

Core Focus Areas

  • Understanding child behavior
  • Emotional regulation skills
  • Healthy communication
  • Consistent structure and routines
  • Positive discipline strategies
  • Trauma-informed parenting
  • Strength-based goal setting

Program Goals

  • Increase parenting confidence
  • Improve parent-child relationships
  • Reduce crisis situations
  • Support healthy development
  • Build coping skills
  • Encourage stability

Who this program helps

  • Parents working through stress
  • Families involved in services
  • Parents learning new skills
  • Caregivers seeking structure
  • Families rebuilding stability
