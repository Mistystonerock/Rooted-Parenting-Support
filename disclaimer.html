Disclaimer

Rooted Parenting Network provides educational information, parenting tools, classroom response guidance, behavior support guidance, and structured family support resources. It is not a substitute for medical care, mental health treatment, legal advice, or emergency services.

Not Clinical Treatment

  • Use of this site does not create a therapist-client, attorney-client, medical, or emergency services relationship.
  • The program is intended for education, support, coaching, and structured parent learning.
  • Families with urgent behavioral health, safety, or crisis concerns should seek help from qualified professionals or emergency services.

Use in Courts, CPS, and Agencies

Program forms, referral materials, tracking tools, and certificates are intended to support documentation and parent education. Agencies and professionals should use independent judgment when applying these tools in legal, child welfare, or service-plan settings.

Emergency Situations

If a child or caregiver is in immediate danger, experiencing a mental health crisis, or facing a medical emergency, contact emergency services or an appropriate crisis support provider right away.
