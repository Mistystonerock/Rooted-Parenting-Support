Helping Kids Manage Anger and Build Self-Worth

One-page summary based only on the worksheet files found in this workspace.

What It Is

This workspace is a collection of printable mental-health worksheets that can help children and teens understand feelings, calm their bodies, and practice healthier thinking. The files suggest a support toolkit for anger control, behavior coaching, and confidence-building activities.

Who It's For

Best for adults helping kids with behavior issues, anger outbursts, low self-esteem, anxiety, ADHD, or trouble expressing emotions.

What It Does

  • Helps kids name feelings and notice anger warning signs.
  • Teaches coping skills like breathing, relaxation, and mindfulness.
  • Uses CBT worksheets to challenge negative thoughts.
  • Supports mood, trigger, and behavior tracking over time.
  • Includes child-centered resources for anger, mindfulness, and emotional regulation.
  • Provides tools adults can use in counseling, home, school, or group support.

How It Works

Components: A flat set of PDF worksheets organized by topic.

Evidence of topics: anger, CBT, mindfulness, coping skills, ADHD, bullying, values, and mood tracking.

Data flow: Adult chooses a worksheet, child completes it on paper or screen, then the adult uses it for discussion and coaching.

App/services: Not found in repo.

How To Use

  • Open the folder of PDFs.
  • Pick a worksheet that matches the child’s current need.
  • Print it or open it in a PDF viewer.
  • Work through it with the child and talk about what they learned.
  • Software setup or start commands: Not found in repo.
