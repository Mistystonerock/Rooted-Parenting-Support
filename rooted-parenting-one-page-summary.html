Rooted Parenting

Trauma-Informed Parenting Support Program

Rooted Parenting is a practical, trauma-informed support program that helps caregivers understand behavior, respond with calm, and build emotional safety at home. It combines parent education, in-the-moment guidance, reflection tools, and printable resources in a simple, accessible format.

Target Population

  • Parents and caregivers of children with behavioral and emotional challenges
  • Families affected by trauma, chronic stress, anxiety, aggression, shutdown, or school-related stress
  • Community programs serving families who need accessible, non-judgmental parenting support

Program Goals

  • Help parents view behavior as communication rather than defiance alone
  • Strengthen calm, connected, trauma-informed responses at home
  • Support caregiver confidence, reflection, and skill-building over time

What Is Included

  • Behavior guides for common parenting challenges
  • Trauma-informed parenting lessons and a structured learning path
  • Parent calm tools, scripts, and co-regulation support
  • Printable worksheets, trackers, planning pages, and reflection tools

Possible Community Uses

  • Parent support programs in schools, family centers, and community agencies
  • Supplemental resources for counselors, social workers, and family support staff
  • Caregiver learning between therapy, case management, or home-visiting sessions

Expected Outcomes

  • Greater parent understanding of trauma, stress, and behavior
  • More consistent use of calm, regulated, non-shaming parenting responses
  • Improved family connection, emotional safety, and repair after hard moments
  • Stronger caregiver readiness to partner with schools and community supports

Summary

Rooted Parenting is designed to be practical, respectful, and easy to use in real-life parenting moments. It is well suited for schools, agencies, family programs, and grant-supported community partnerships focused on child well-being, caregiver support, and family resilience.
