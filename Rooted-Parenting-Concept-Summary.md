# Rooted Parenting Concept Summary

## Problem Statement

Many parents and caregivers are supporting children whose behavior is shaped by chronic stress, trauma exposure, anxiety, emotional dysregulation, school difficulties, or relationship strain. In many communities, families are offered behavior-focused advice that may not explain what is happening underneath a child’s reactions or how to respond in ways that build safety, trust, and long-term skills. Parents often need practical, accessible, non-judgmental support that helps them respond effectively during stressful moments while also strengthening family relationships over time.

## Solution Overview

Rooted Parenting is a trauma-informed parenting support app designed to help caregivers understand behavior as communication and respond with calm, supportive, skill-building guidance. The app combines in-the-moment behavior guides, structured parent learning lessons, regulation tools, school support resources, and printable worksheets in a simple mobile-friendly format. Its purpose is to translate trauma-informed principles into clear, everyday parenting support that families can use at home, in school partnerships, and in community settings.

## Target Population

Rooted Parenting is designed for:

- Parents and caregivers of children with behavioral and emotional challenges
- Families affected by trauma, chronic stress, anxiety, school refusal, aggression, shutdown, or conflict
- Caregivers seeking practical tools that are supportive rather than punitive
- Community-based family support programs, parent educators, schools, counseling programs, and pediatric behavioral health partners

## Core Features

- Behavior support guides for common concerns such as anger, anxiety, defiance, school refusal, aggression, shutdown, lying, homework conflict, peer conflict, and sibling conflict
- Ready-to-use parent scripts for stressful moments, including what to say and what to avoid
- Trauma-informed learning content that explains how stress and trauma affect children’s behavior
- A structured Rooted Parenting learning path with step-by-step lessons and reflection prompts
- Parent calm tools focused on grounding, breathing, co-regulation, and self-regulation reminders
- Printable worksheets for reflection, planning, school meetings, trigger tracking, and caregiver regulation
- Beginner-friendly mobile design intended for quick access during real-life parenting moments

## Expected Outcomes

Rooted Parenting is intended to support:

- Increased parent understanding of behavior as communication
- More consistent use of calm, regulated, non-shaming responses
- Improved parent confidence during emotionally intense moments
- Better family-school communication around behavioral and emotional needs
- Stronger emotional safety, connection, and repair within families
- Increased caregiver access to trauma-informed parenting education in an easy-to-use format

## Possible Community Use Cases

- Parent support programs operated by schools, family resource centers, and community nonprofits
- Supplemental family tools for school counselors, social workers, and behavioral support teams
- Parent education groups focused on trauma-informed care, emotional regulation, or family resilience
- Resource support for pediatric clinics, mental health providers, and care coordination programs
- Home-based caregiver learning between counseling or family support sessions

## Brief Implementation Plan

### Phase 1: Pilot Launch

- Finalize Version 1 as a mobile-friendly web app
- Test the app with a small group of parents, caregivers, or community partners
- Gather feedback on clarity, usefulness, ease of use, and priority content gaps

### Phase 2: Community Partner Use

- Share the app with schools, family support organizations, and behavioral health partners
- Add referral workflows, printable resource packets, and optional guided onboarding for partner programs
- Develop simple tracking for resource use and parent feedback

### Phase 3: Growth and Iteration

- Expand behavior guides, school partnership tools, and learning lessons
- Add multilingual access, accessibility improvements, and optional account-based progress tracking
- Explore partnership models for community implementation, training, and ongoing resource support

## Summary

Rooted Parenting is a practical, trauma-informed support tool that helps caregivers move from reaction to understanding, from shame to connection, and from crisis response to skill-building. It is well suited for grant, pilot, and community partnership conversations focused on family resilience, caregiver support, and child emotional well-being.
